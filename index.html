Top 5 Quotes from your favourite games

Hearing some motivational lines from your elders,teachers or your favourite movie actors is one way. But have you ever heard some
from the games you play? Hear are some strong lines we came across from the games we play.

  1. God of war: Ragnarok

    "Wars Are Won By Those Willing To Sacrifice Everything."

  2. GTA-V

    "At Least Respect What You Do, Who You Are. If You Let That Idiot Die, You A Bigger A**hole Than I Thought." - Tanisha Jackson

  3. Call Of Duty: Modern Warfare 2

    “History Is Written By The Victor. History Is Filled With Liars. If He Lives, And We Die, His Truth Becomes Written And Ours Is Lost.” - Captain Price

  4. Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice

    "Hesitation Is Defeat."

  5. Spider-Man 2

    “You Always Have A Choice.”
